Overview of Nuclear Physics

This section covers

This project consists of two different visualizations. One visualization is based on each plants capacity factor. The capacity factor is a percentage value that indicates at what capacity the plant is generating power relative to its own technological, physical, and/or other constraints. The second, more complex visualization shows how this capacity factor changes per month for each power plant during the years 2014-2020. This project consists of over 10,000 lines of code and the end product is displayed on the CEC's official website.
