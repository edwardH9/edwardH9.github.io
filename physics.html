Analysis of Monatomic (Single Basis) Nanowire

Considering a wire one atom thick, composed completely of the same atom. A tight binding model can be constructed with the following Hamiltonian: $$H = \sum\limits_{i=1}^n{{eC_i^\dagger}{C_i}}+\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n-1} t({{C_i}{C_{i+1}^\dagger}+{C_{i+1}^\dagger}{C_i}}) .$$

    Where:
  1. C terms are electron creation/annihilation operators
  2. t denotes the quantum mechanical tunneling probability, in this case modulated by electrical current
  3. e is the onsite energy of any site in the wire
  4. n is the index of any atom in the wire
